About H&M

Welcome to H&M, a used dealership with a revolutionary mission: to get everyone their dream car, regardless of their financial background or credit history.

Located in Elizabeth, NJ, we believe that reliable transportation shouldn't be a privilege reserved for the few. Our Buy Here Pay Here financing model ensures that we can say "yes" when traditional dealerships say "no."

At H&M, we're not just selling cars – we're providing opportunities, building relationships, and helping our community move forward. Your dream car is waiting, and we're here to make it a reality. Our knowledgeable sales staff will help answer your questions and find the perfect vehicle for you and your family, with financing options that work for your situation.
